Bucket-List


An Easy Way To Track Your Goals and Dreams.








How To Use


You can add your tasks and goals to your bucket by simply writing it into the section provided at your homepage.







Features


  • You can add your tasks and goals to your bucket by simply writing it into the section provided at your homepage.
  • You can update your tasks by clicking on update button and change or update your goal and also mark it as completed and incomplete.
  • You can delete the tasks which you don't like or added them to the bucket accidently.
  • You can add a category to your task and filter them accordingly to ease your experience of finding the tasks.
